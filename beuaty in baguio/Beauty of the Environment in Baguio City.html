Beauty of the Environment in Baguio City

Baguio City is famous from its beautiful surrounding, cool and refreshing weather, rich culture, and kind people. From this traits tourist around the world travel to Baguio City to see its beauty, and called as the “Summer Capital of the Philippines.” But one of its prime beauty is the environment, the tall pine trees that surround the city with fresh air, the colorful flower that lighten the area, cool and refreshing air that attract many people is just one of the beauty of the environment in Baguio City.

Baguio City is also known to be “the city of pines” because a large number of pine trees covered almost the whole city that give refreshing air in the surrounding. The city also surrounded by colorful flowers that can only find in the high places like Baguio City. Overall, Baguio City have one of the most beautiful environment in the Philippines and also give benefit to the people because of tourist yearly.
