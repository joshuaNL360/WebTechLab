Baguio


The Beautiful Baguio City

Baguio City ,also known as the “Summer Capital of the Philippines”, is famous for its beautiful surroundings, cool and refreshing weather, rich culture, and kind people. Because of these, tourist around the world travel to Baguio City to see its beauty. But one of its prime beauty is the environment, the tall pine trees that surround the city with fresh air, the colorful flower that lighten the area, and breathtaking mountain ranges are just few of the many fascinations in Baguio City.

Baguio City is also known to be “the city of pines” because a large number of pine trees covers almost the whole city. The city is also surrounded by rare flowers that can only be found in high places like Baguio City. Overall, Baguio City has one of the most beautiful environment in the Philippines.

BAGUIO CITY TODAY

Baguio City today has become more modernized than before. Slowly, it has been keeping up with the changes over the century. In the recent years, Baguio City's signature image as the "Summer Capital of the Philippines" is slowly becoming a thing in the past now because of the rising temperature which made it not-so cool and refreshing anymore.

One common cause of this is POLLUTION. With the tourists that comes to visit, together with the other irresponsible residents of the city, Baguio City has been polluted through the air, water and eveon on land. Many people are now, moving into the city and one of their choices is here in Baguio. With the increase of people, there was a need to build more homes to accommodate them.

If these types of pollution continues, the city will slowly lose it's title and also the residents will also suffer the consequences. There might be a rise in the percentage of unhealthy residents. The local government is taking actions by implementing different policies and orders to help preserve our environment. We hope that as good citizens of the country, we follow these policies and be a role model to the youth.
